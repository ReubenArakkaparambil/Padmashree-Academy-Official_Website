Privacy Policy

We take the privacy and security of our students and their parents' personal information very seriously. This privacy policy outlines the measures we take to protect your personal information.

1.Personal Information Collected
We may collect the following personal information from you when you enroll in our courses or use our website:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Payment information
  • Academic and educational background

2.Personal Information Collected
How we use your personal information We use your personal information to:
  • Provide our courses and services to you
  • Communicate with you about your enrollment and our services
  • Process your payments
  • Improve our courses and services
  • Send you promotional materials about our courses and services

3.Storage and Security of Personal Information
How we store and secure your personal information We store your personal information securely and take appropriate measures to protect it from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We use industry-standard encryption technologies to protect your personal information when it is transmitted over the internet.

4.Sharing of Personal Information
How we share your personal information,We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except in the following circumstances:
  • To our trusted partners and service providers who assist us in providing our courses and services to you
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations

5.Requests for Access or Deletion of Personal Information
How we handle requests to access or delete your personal information You have the right to access or delete your personal information that we have collected. To request access or deletion of your personal information, please contact us using the contact information provided below.

6.Changes to privacy policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices and services. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.


If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy or our handling of your personal information, please contact us here.
