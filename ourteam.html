Our Team

Shruti Gosavi (B.E Computers)

Co-Founder and Teacher

After a stint of 6 years in the IT Industry, Shruti gave in to her heart's calling and became a teacher. She has been teaching, guiding and mentoring students for the last 11 years now. She has the ability to go down to the level of the students, which helps her to gel well with them. She uses unconventional methods to make her subjects more practical, understandable and enjoyable to the students.




Sagar Gosavi (B.E. EXTC)

Technology Head

Sagar is a perfectionist with a sharp sense of technology and its applications. It was this knowledge and experience that helped the academy make a smooth transition from offline classes, to online classes, during the unprecedented pandemic situation. His ability to evaluate situations and plan in advance has time and again proven to be one of the strong pillars of support, for the Academy.




Swati Dahasahasra (B.Com, LLB)

Academics and Planning Head

An experience of nearly two decades, in numerous institutes, is what makes Swati a thorough professional. She has successfully essayed various roles in all these years. She has 10 years of experience in Academics Management & Business Development. She has conducted Teachers Training Programme, Student-Parent Counselling, and has also been a Moderator & Guest Lecturer throughout Mumbai. She is an ace planner which makes her a strong guiding force for the Academy. A brilliant teacher that she is, helps her bring out the best in her students.




Shweta Kapur (B.E)

Finance and PR Head

Shweta has an illustrious experience of working in the banking domain for more than 5 years along with having an innate sense of money. Her vibrant personality helps her in building great relations with the people she interacts with. All these qualities make her the most apt person in charge of Finances and PR.




Aarya Gore (B.E. Computers)

Training Head

Aarya is a B. Pharm who chose to use her knowledge for the benefit of students, rather than going the corporate way. She is a Chemistry faculty for ICSE, IG, IB, CBSE and State boards in leading educational institutions in and around Mumbai. She has worked as a Science HOD and as an Academic Head.
