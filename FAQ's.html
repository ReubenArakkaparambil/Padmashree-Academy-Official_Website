Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)



What courses do you offer?

We offer classes for students of grades 7th to 10th, of SSC, CBSE and ICSE boards.

What is the duration of your courses?

The duration of the regular classes is throughout the academic year. But we also conduct short duration workshops that run for a few weeks at a time.

Who are your faculty members?

We have separate faculty for different subjects. All of our faculty members are passionate teachers with over a decade long experience of teaching/coaching hundreds of students.

What teaching methods do you use?

While we do believe in the traditional chalk-board method of teaching, as it has been proven that the best learning happens only when the student is physically present in front of the teacher. Having said that, we do employ contemporary techniques like educational videos, hands-on experiences and flipped classrooms to take the learning experience to the next level. We are also open to the online mode of teaching, whenever the opportunity presents itself.

What is the fee structure for your courses?

The fees structure varies according to the standard and the board. For further details, please get in touch with us.

What is the success rate of your students?

Success is hardly quantifiable, as it is relative to every person. But, if we were to measure success in terms of the performance of the students in the boards, then we are proud to say that we have always been massively successful. For more details, please go to the achievers page.

How can I enroll in your courses?

For further details, please get in touch with us.

What are your classroom timings?

All our classes start from 2:30 pm onwards. For more details, please get in touch with us.

Do you provide study material?

We only provide important points or cheat sheets etc as study material. The rest of the material is created by the students, in the class, in the presence of their teacher.
